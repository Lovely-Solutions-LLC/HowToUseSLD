How To Use SLD

Step One

After you've installed the app and opened up the Status Label Directory board view, you will see the name of each status column on your board listed out. Click on any of the dropdown arrows to open up the labels associated with that column.

Step Two

The first editable field from the left is the notes field. Clicking on the circled page icon for a label will open up it's "Notes" page.

Step Three

The "Notes" page provides users with a user-friendly rich text editor that allows for detailed and well-organized documentation to be compiled.

Step Four

The "Link" field provides users with a space to add links to any outside materials associated with a label, such as a Google Doc, or Loom video. After entering a link, the user can click on the blue "Go" button to immediately be taken to the link that is placed in that field.

Step Five

Both the "Last Updated By" field and the "Last Updated" field will update automatically based on when, and by who any field in the directory is modified.

Step Six

Each label will have a small eye icon to the far right of the view. Clicking that icon will hide the label from the list. A use case for hiding a label might be if it's a label that doesn't require any documentation, so it's better off hidden away and not cluttering the list.

Once a label for a column is hidden, a new button will appear underneath the list of labels for that column that says "Show Hidden Labels". Clicking on this will unhide all labels that are hidden for this list.
